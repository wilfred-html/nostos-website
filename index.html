Brand story

Food inspired by memory

Nostos comes from the Greek idea of returning home. That theme already sits at the centre of how they describe themselves: every plate, every flavour, every detail is tied to memory, craft, and roots.

The strongest public signals we could recover point to a restaurant that isn’t trying to be loud or trendy. It feels intimate, personal, and proudly Greek — the kind of place where hospitality matters as much as the menu.

That’s the direction of this site: less “flashy restaurant marketing”, more soulful coastal dining room with a strong sense of origin.

Recovered brand language
“Our food is inspired by memory: every plate, every flavour, has a purpose and a story.”

Other confirmed cues from public sources: “A return home”, “Authentic Greek restaurant in Hout Bay”, and “Run by two Authentic Greeks”. Those lines shaped the tone, palette, and structure of this first version.

Aesthetic direction

Warm, coastal, understated

This concept leans into sun-faded stone, olive tones, terracotta warmth, and editorial typography — a digital atmosphere that feels closer to a Greek lunch unfolding slowly in Hout Bay than a generic booking page.

A return home

A Greek restaurant with warmth, soul, and no need to shout.

Nostos takes its name from the idea of returning home. That feeling should shape the whole experience — familiar without being tired, refined without becoming cold, and memorable without turning theatrical.

The site should reflect that same restraint: beautiful typography, warm Mediterranean tones, direct booking, and just enough story to make the place feel real before someone even arrives.

The experience

What the site should communicate immediately

Not design commentary. Not placeholder language. Just the essentials of a real restaurant brand: what this place is, how it feels, why it’s worth visiting, and how to book without friction.
